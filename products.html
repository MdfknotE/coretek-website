Products

We offer raw yarns with Dyneema® (including staple fiber and filament fiber), customized yarn developments (Dyneema® Combined Yarn), and Dyneema® Composite Fabric.

Staple Fiber

Dyneema® Raw Yarn

Blending Dyneema® with different fibers to elevate fabric pilling and abrasion performance, suitable for outdoor and workwear clothing, footwear and so on.

Available Specs: STW38/1 (white color/1dpf), STW38/2 (white color/2dpf), STB38/2 (black color/2dpf)

Filament Fiber

Dyneema® Raw Yarn

Super strong continous filament fiber to elevate strength for ultimate abrasion and cut resistance. Possible applications are clothing, shoe upper, protection gear.

Available Specs: 400d, 200d, 150d, 100d, diamond grade (cur resistant)

Dyneema® Combined Yarn

We offer customized yarn development, combining Dyneema® with cotton, polyester, nylon, TENCEL and more. Yarn types includes covered yarn, ring spun yarn and so on.

Dyneema® Composite Fabric

Non-woven composite utilizing Dyneema® fibers, with strength to weight ratio 15x stronger than steel (and it floats on water). A perfect solution for outdoor sports gear such as jackets, backpacks, ultralight tents, lightweight/waterproof tarps, shoes etc..
